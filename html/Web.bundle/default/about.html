About

Welcome to the revised NHS Safeguarding App; this iteration includes links to trusted safeguarding partners and updated content based on any new safeguarding legislation.

The app has been developed with safeguarding colleagues to act as a comprehensive resource for healthcare professionals, carers and citizens to increase their awareness and understanding of safeguarding.

Thank you for all you do every day to safeguard every citizen.
