The voice of lived experience, victims and survivors

The voice of the lived experience, victims and survivors has been crucial in shaping the narrative and social movement of NHS safeguarding.

It has created a dialogue for safeguarding system leaders that is trauma informed, strength based and authentic to the lived experience and has been crucial in shaping the key priorities for the NHS safeguarding programmes of work.
